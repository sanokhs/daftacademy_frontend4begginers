About

Straight from Lublin

6BM is a young which has it's origins in the city of Lublin, playing music described as electrorock. Band members aim to convey emotions, reflexion but also hope.

They are inspired by various genres, like: alternative rock, nu-metal, synthwave or even drum'n'bass. As for now they relesed their debuty EP - TRINITY and they started working on their first full lenght album.
